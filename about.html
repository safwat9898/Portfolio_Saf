Nice to Meet You!

Hello, I'm Safwat, a passionate Visual & Ui Designer focused on creating impactful and empathetic digital experiences. Currently, I'm studying at SFU's Interactive Arts and Technology program. Throughout my journey, I've explored a wide range of disciplines, from communication to computer science, human-computer interaction, and interactive systems. I aim to integrate these diverse skills into a holistic design approach.

My goal is simple: using design as a tool for change and fostering genuine connections. I love light, mellow contrasting with vibrant colors which aligns with my daily stylewear and I try to recreate that through my design so people can visualize and understand my personality through my design. Also a big fan of reading books and manga which enriches my understanding of context and storytelling, helping me create meaningful and engaging experiences.I strive to bring these influences into my work, crafting designs that are both visually stunning and deeply resonant.
